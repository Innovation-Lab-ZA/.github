## Getting Started

1. **New here?** Introduce yourself in [Discussions](https://github.com/orgs/Innovation-Lab-ZA/discussions) — we don't bite
2. **Looking for a project?** Check the [Projects](https://github.com/orgs/Innovation-Lab-ZA/projects) tab
3. **Stuck on a setup?** Read the repo's README first, then ask in Discussions
4. **Competition questions?** Contact your team lead or open a Discussion
